Trade missions for startups and scale-ups

Last checked on:
20 June 2025
Is part of:
  • Innovation, research and patents
  • International business and export

Do you want to showcase your product and meet with potential investors, partners and customers? Join one of our startup trade missions to the world's most significant tech exhibitions.

Events and trade mission worldwide

Are you interested in international opportunities and challenges for your startup or scale-up? Then pre-register as a startup, scale-up, partner or supporter for one of our missions to the leading tech exhibitions. Attending these missions is a great way to boost your business, meet exciting people and discover new opportunities.

Startups

Would you like to stay informed about future missions or pre-register for one or more of them? Please complete our form, and we will notify you when you can register for these missions. By pre-registering for trade missions, you will be among the first to receive more information as soon as it becomes available.

Partners and supporters

You can also contribute to one or more missions as a partner or supporter. Please complete our form and tell us how you can support us.
