Austria Wirtschaftsservice Gesellschaft mbH (aws) is the promotional bank of the Austrian federal government and is 100% owned by the Republic of Austria. The Federal Ministry of Economy, Energy and Tourism (BMWET) and the Federal Ministry of Innovation, Mobility and Infrastructur (BMIMI) act as its owner representatives.
